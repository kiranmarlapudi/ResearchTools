Criteria pairwise comparison – For each pair, select which criterion is more important, then use the slider to indicate how much more important (1 = equal, 9 = extreme). If you see a highlighted row, the consistency between your answers is low – please adjust.
Some pairs are inconsistent – highlighted rows suggest changes.
Alternatives assessment – Based on your criteria and sub‑criteria comparisons, we compute how each alternative performs on each sub‑criterion. This shows which alternative works best for each sub‑criterion and why.
